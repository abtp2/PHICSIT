About Code Sprint - 0.1

Code Sprint is back with a bang!! We are organizing a Hackathon to encourage innovation and entrepreneurship among the BS community. Our Hackathon consists of 3 rounds: Ideation Round, Prototyping Round (online), and a 36-hour Grand Finale at IITM Campus. Shortlisting after each round. We have a variety of pre-hackathon learning sessions in different domains.
The Ideation Round- This includes analysing real-world problems and their idea pitching.

The Prototyping Round - The Jury will analyse submitted ideas and select projects for the Grand Finale Round. Each team will have 10 minutes to present its prototype online. Following that, selected teams will receive mentoring to develop products based on their ideas.

Grand finale-Top teams from the Prototyping Round will compete at the IITM campus. Teams will be selected to showcase their projects based on business potential and scalability. IIT Madras members and industry experts judge the competition, and the top three teams will be announced as winners. Jury might become the first people to invest if they really love your product app and find it interesting.
