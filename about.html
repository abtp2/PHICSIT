About Us

Phicsit is a community dedicated to helping people learn the tech skills needed to succeed in the ever-evolving digital landscape. Our goal is to empower individuals by providing a platform for learning, collaboration, and growth.
Through our content, we aim to break down complex concepts and make them accessible to everyone. Our community is made up of people from diverse backgrounds and skill levels, and we believe that everyone can benefit from learning tech skills.
Whether you are an aspiring programmer, designer, or just curious about the tech industry, we welcome you to join our community. Follow us on Twitter to stay up-to-date with our latest content, participate in our events, and connect with like-minded individuals. Let's learn and grow together at Phicsit.

Mission

According to the Bureau of Labor Statistics, jobs in the computer and information technology field are projected to grow 11% from 2019 to 2029, much faster than the average for all occupations.
The National Science Foundation reports that there is a growing demand for workers with expertise in artificial intelligence, data science, and cybersecurity.
Despite this demand, many people lack access to the resources and training needed to develop these skills.
At Phicsit, our mission is to bridge this skills gap by providing a community platform for learning and collaboration. We aim to empower individuals with the skills they need to succeed in the digital age.

Vision

According to a study by Burning Glass Technologies, nearly two-thirds of all entry-level IT jobs require a Bachelor's degree or higher, making it difficult for people without formal education to enter the field. The same study found that many employers are looking for candidates with specific tech skills, such as programming languages, software development, and database management. The World Economic Forum predicts that by 2025, the top emerging jobs will require skills in areas such as artificial intelligence, cloud computing, and cybersecurity. At Phicsit, our vision is to provide a community where anyone can learn the skills needed to thrive in the tech industry. We aim to make tech education more accessible and inclusive, and to help individuals develop the skills that are most in-demand by employers.
