About Us

Welcome to PHICSIT community! We are a dedicated group of professionals who believe in the power of connection. Our mission is to bridge the gap between skilled individuals and companies/startups looking for talented individuals to join their teams.

We understand that finding the right job, internship, or freelance opportunity can be a daunting task, and our goal is to make that process easier for you. Our platform provides a space for skilled individuals to showcase their talents and connect with potential employers.

We firmly believe that everyone deserves an opportunity to pursue their passions and reach their full potential. That's why we work tirelessly to ensure that our community members have access to valuable resources, including professional development opportunities, networking events, and mentorship programs.

Whether you're a recent graduate looking for your first job or an experienced professional seeking new challenges, we invite you to join us and become part of our thriving community. Together, we can help you achieve your career goals and build a brighter future.

Our Mission

According to the Bureau of Labor Statistics, jobs in the computer and information technology field are projected to grow 11% from 2019 to 2029, much faster than the average for all occupations.

The National Science Foundation reports that there is a growing demand for workers with expertise in artificial intelligence, data science, and cybersecurity.

Despite this demand, many people lack access to the resources and training needed to develop these skills. One major barrier to accessing resources is cost. Training programs and courses can be expensive, making it difficult for individuals with limited financial resources to participate.

At PHICSIT, our mission is to bridge this skills gap by providing a community platform for learning and collaboration. We aim to empower individuals with the skills they need to succeed in the digital age.

Our Vision

According to a study by Burning Glass Technologies, nearly two-thirds of all entry-level IT jobs require a Bachelor's degree or higher, making it difficult for people without formal education to enter the field.

The same study found that many employers are looking for candidates with specific tech skills, such as programming languages, software development, and database management.

The World Economic Forum predicts that by 2025, the top emerging jobs will require skills in areas such as artificial intelligence, cloud computing, and cybersecurity.

At PHICSIT, our vision is to provide a community where anyone can learn the skills needed to thrive in the tech industry. We aim to make tech education more accessible and inclusive, and to help individuals develop the skills that are most in-demand by employers.
