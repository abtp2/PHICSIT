Community Guidelines

PHICSIT is committed to being a safe and supportive for students as well as companies.
By using PHICSIT you agree to follow these guidelines and adhere to ourÂ Terms of Service.

When using PHICSIT, please remember to:

  • Respectful communication : We expect all members of our community to communicate in a respectful and civil manner. It's important to treat others as you want to be treated. Encouraging hatred or violence on the basis of race, ethnicity, religion, gender, age, sexual orientation, or disability is completely unacceptable on PHICSIT, as is creating content that threatens or harasses another person or group. We do not tolerate bullying, racism, or discrimination for any reason.
  • No spamming : Please do not use our platform to spam other users with unsolicited messages or promotions.
  • Protect user privacy : Do not share any personal information of other users without their consent. This includes email addresses, phone numbers, and other contact details. You can read our full privacy policy here.
  • Legal compliance : All members of our community must abide by applicable laws and regulations. Any illegal activity will not be tolerated.
  • Feedback and suggestions : We welcome feedback and suggestions from our community members. Please feel free to share your ideas for improving our website.
  • Enjoyment : We want our community members to enjoy using our site and interacting with one another. Let's make this a positive and welcoming environment for everyone.

By following these guidelines, we can create a welcoming and respectful environment where everyone can learn and grow together.
